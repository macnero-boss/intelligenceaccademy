Intelligence Accademy is a leading educational institution that offers a holistic and innovative curriculum for students from kindergarten to JHS 3. Our school is located in a spacious and green campus, equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and resources to support our students' academic, physical, and social development.

Facilities

At Intelligence Accademy, we believe that a conducive learning environment is essential for our students' success. That's why we have invested in modern and well-maintained facilities, such as:

- Smart classrooms with interactive whiteboards and multimedia projectors
- A fully-stocked library with a wide range of books, magazines, and online resources
- A computer lab with high-speed internet access and the latest software
- A science lab with advanced equipment and materials for hands-on experiments
- A music room with various instruments and a recording studio
- A art room with a variety of tools and materials for creative expression
- A gymnasium with a basketball court, a volleyball court, and a fitness center
- A cafeteria with healthy and delicious food options
- A playground with swings, slides, and climbing structures
- A medical clinic with a qualified nurse and a doctor on call

Clubs

    At Intelligence Accademy, we encourage our students to explore their interests and talents beyond the classroom. That's why we offer a range of extracurricular activities, such as:
    - Academic clubs, such as math club, science club, chess club, and debate club
    - Arts clubs, such as drama club, dance club, choir club, and photography club
    - Sports clubs, such as soccer club, basketball club, tennis club, and swimming club
    - Service clubs, such as environmental club, animal welfare club, peer tutoring club, and community service club
    Our students can join any club they like, or even start their own club with the guidance of our teachers. Our clubs meet regularly and organize various events and projects throughout the year.

COMPETITIONS

    At Intelligence Accademy, we challenge our students to excel in their chosen fields and showcase their skills and achievements. That's why we participate in various local, national, and international competitions, such as:

    - Math Olympiad, a prestigious contest for math enthusiasts
    - Science Fair, a creative platform for scientific inquiry and innovation
    - Spelling Bee, a fun and rewarding test of vocabulary and spelling
    - Poetry Slam, a dynamic and expressive performance of original poems
    - Basketball Tournament, a competitive and exciting game of teamwork and sportsmanship
    - Music Festival, a colorful and festive celebration of music and culture

    Our students have won many awards and accolades in these competitions, and we are proud of their accomplishments.

HOUSES

    At Intelligence Accademy, we foster a sense of community and belonging among our students. That's why we have four houses, each with its own name, color, and mascot. Our houses are:

    - Red House, with the lion as the mascot
    - Blue House, with the eagle as the mascot
    - Green House, with the snake as the mascot
    - Yellow House, with the tiger as the mascot

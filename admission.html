Our Enrolment Process

  • 1. Inquire and Gather Information.
    Parents or guardians can inquire about enrollment by contacting our admission office via phone or email.
    Our staff will provide information about the school, curriculum, and admission requirements.
  • 2. Schedule a Visit.
    Interested families are encouraged to schedule a campus visit.
    During the visit, parents and students can explore our facilities, meet teachers, and get a feel for the school environment.
  • 3. Submit Application.
    Parents can obtain the admission application form from our website or directly from the school office.
    Complete the form with accurate information and attach any required documents (birth certificate, previous school records, etc.)..
  • 4. Entrance Assessment (if applicable).
    Depending on the grade level, students may need to take an entrance assessment or placement test.
    The assessment helps us understand the student’s academic level and tailor our support accordingly.
  • 5. Interview (if applicable).
    Some grade levels may require an interview with the student and parents.
    The interview allows us to learn more about the student’s interests, goals, and any special needs.
  • 6. Admission Decision.
    Our admission committee reviews each application.
    Parents will receive an admission decision via email or phone.
  • 7. Acceptance and Enrollment.
    If accepted, parents will receive an acceptance letter.
    Pay the registration fee to secure the student’s spot.

We look forward to welcoming new learners to our school community! If you have any questions, feel free to contact our admission office.
